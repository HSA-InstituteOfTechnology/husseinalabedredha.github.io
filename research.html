Research & Working Papers

My research focuses on housing economics, financial risk behavior under uncertainty, land-use constraints, and the application of econometric tools to real-world policy and market questions.

Working Papers

Did Supply Constraints Amplify Housing Price Growth in DFW?

An econometric investigation using IV, 2SLS, and constraint metrics to evaluate the structural limitations influencing price dynamics during COVID-era demand shocks.

Hedging Behavior of Upstream O&G Firms Under Climate Policy Uncertainty

Examines risk-management behavior and investment sensitivity using firm-level hedging data, futures curves, and policy-driven volatility measures.
